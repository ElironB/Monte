## Reflections on Contradictions

*Ambitious *product launch* vs. **procrastination** on marketing docs.*  
I set a goal to launch MVP by end‑March, but kept putting off the docs. I realized that my *product‑minded* nature pushes me to get something out *quickly*, yet I’m afraid of “looking unprofessional” if the docs are poor.

*Disciplined saving* vs. *spending on side projects.*  
I’m proud of the 10 % monthly savings, but the side CLI tool costs $30/month. I’m tempted to treat it as a hobby, but it also fuels curiosity—how do I reconcile the *“save for future”* vs. *“invest in learning”* tension?

*Curiosity* vs. *Focus.*  
Every new framework or design pattern fascinates me. Yet each curiosity leads to a new side task that pulls me away from the MVP. I’ve started blocking 2 hrs on Tuesdays for “learning” and the rest for “delivery” to keep the two worlds separated.
